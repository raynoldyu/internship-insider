Internship Success Guide

Everything you need to know to land and excel in your dream internship.

Before the Internship

Resume & Application Tips

  • Tailor your resume for each position, highlighting relevant skills and experiences
  • Use action verbs and quantify achievements (e.g., "Increased efficiency by 25%")
  • Keep your resume to one page and use a clean, professional format
  • Proofread multiple times and have others review it
  • Include relevant coursework, projects, and extracurricular activities

Interview Preparation

  • Research the company thoroughly - know their products, culture, and recent news
  • Practice common behavioral questions using the STAR method
  • Prepare thoughtful questions to ask the interviewer
  • Dress professionally and arrive early
  • Follow up with a thank-you email within 24 hours

During the Internship

First Week Success

  • Arrive early and stay organized
  • Take detailed notes during training and meetings
  • Ask questions and seek clarification when needed
  • Introduce yourself to team members and other interns
  • Set up regular check-ins with your manager

Professional Development

  • Take initiative and volunteer for projects
  • Build relationships with colleagues across departments
  • Attend company events and networking opportunities
  • Keep a journal of your accomplishments and learnings
  • Seek feedback regularly and implement it

Work-Life Balance

  • Set clear boundaries between work and personal time
  • Take regular breaks to maintain productivity
  • Stay organized and prioritize tasks effectively
  • Communicate openly about workload and deadlines
  • Make time for networking and social activities

End of Internship

Making a Lasting Impression

  • Complete all projects and document your work thoroughly
  • Schedule exit interviews and gather feedback
  • Connect with colleagues on LinkedIn
  • Write thank-you notes to key mentors and managers
  • Update your resume with new skills and accomplishments

Full-Time Conversion

  • Express interest in full-time opportunities early
  • Understand the conversion process and timeline
  • Build a strong case for why you should be hired
  • Maintain relationships with decision-makers
  • Have a backup plan if conversion isn't possible
